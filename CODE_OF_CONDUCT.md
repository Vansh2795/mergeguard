# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Please read the full text at the link above. In summary, we are committed to
providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We expect all
participants to act professionally and respectfully.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project
maintainers at **conduct@mergeguard.dev**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
